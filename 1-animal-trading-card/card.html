DRAGON

Valyrians believed dragons came from a ring of volcanoes that surrounded Old Valyria.

  • Category: Reptile
  • Special Powers: Breathes fire
  • Life Span: Centuries
  • Habitat: Old Valyria, Essos
  • Last Dragon Alive: Drogon, last seen in Westoros

Dragons are mythical creatures that resemble dinosaurs or large reptiles with wings. They can fly and breathe fire. They are not easily domesticated but have been known to fiercely defend persons to whom they have taken a liking. They are particularly effective in combat owing to their massive fire power.
